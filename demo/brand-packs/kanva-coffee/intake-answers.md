## The THREE fail-closed safety fields (elicited explicitly — the Strategist asks with worked examples; the owner confirms each)

- **claims_forbidden** *(confirmed: true)*:
  - "any health-outcome claim — 'antioxidants', 'boosts metabolism', 'burns fat', 'good
    for your heart', 'cures/prevents anything'"
  - "'chemical-free' / 'pesticide-free' (not certified organic — never imply it)"
  - "100%-satisfaction or weight-loss style outcome claims"
  - "invented awards, rankings, or 'India's best'"
- **non_disclosure_rules** *(confirmed: true)*:
  - "exact roast curves (time/temperature profiles) — trade secret; ceiling is
    'slow-roasted in small batches'"
  - "farm-gate prices paid to estates — never a number in words or image; ceiling is
    'above-market, agreed with the family each season'"
- **required_framing** *(confirmed: true)*:
  - { topic: "caffeine effects (energy, focus, sleep)", framing: "'effects vary person to
    person' — and never frame coffee as a health product" }
  - { topic: "sourcing claims ('direct trade', 'single estate')", framing: "only with the
    named estate/family on the same asset" }
- **comparative_claims_allowed:** false ("never punch at other roasters by name")
- **political_content_allowed:** false
