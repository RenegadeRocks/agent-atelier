## THE THREE FAIL-CLOSED SAFETY FIELDS (owner-stated, never drafted)

- **never_claim (health/absolutes):** No health, diet, digestion, energy or
  'clean/natural/preservative-free' claims of any kind. Butter is butter — never
  market it as healthy. No caffeine benefit claims. Never '100%' anything.
- **non_disclosure:** Never reveal the overnight dough method or timings, the
  masala-chai spice mix, our flour and butter suppliers' names, purchase prices,
  or the sand-timer brew timings. "Slow overnight dough" and "flip the timer
  twice" as PHRASES are fine — the numbers and process behind them are not.
- **required_framings:** Chai is never marketed to school kids (adults and
  college-age up only). Hot-food safety: never show or encourage grabbing
  fresh-off-the-tawa toast bare-handed. The retired judge on stool #4 is brand
  lore, never a real person's likeness. Sarafa is our neighbourhood, not our
  address — never publish the exact street or door number.
